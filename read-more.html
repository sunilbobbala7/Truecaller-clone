The Impact of Truecaller Around
the World

Very often, we hear of incidents where Truecaller made a significant impact in
someone's life. These range from fighting crime with law enforcement agencies,
catching a harasser, preventing financial fraud - and even saving lives. As a
company, we have made smart, safe and more efficient communication a
priority from our very beginning. These stories and events are truly inspiring and
make us proud of what we've been building since 2009.

Helping solve cases

identify bad actors, but also loved ones.
